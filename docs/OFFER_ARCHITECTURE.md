**MIRD's position: We never touch agent fees. That's entirely the team leader's business.**

| Team Leader Action | What They Can Do |
|---|---|
| Charge agents $97/mo | Modest - helps offset platform cost |
| Charge agents $147/mo | 10 agents = $1,470/mo — nearly covers Starter |
| Charge agents $300/mo | 10 agents = $3,000/mo — covers most of Growth |
| Charge agents $500/mo | 10 agents = $5,000/mo — fully covers Growth |
| Charge agents $800/mo | 10 agents = $8,000/mo — covers Scale + profit |

**The math that closes the deal:**
> "If an agent gets just one extra closed deal per month from the system, they've paid for their seat 10–50x over. Whatever you charge them — $97 or $800 — they're still wildly ahead. And you're not paying for the system anymore. Your team is."

**MIRD's revenue is clean:** We charge the team leader. What happens between them and their agents is their business, their profit, their margin.
